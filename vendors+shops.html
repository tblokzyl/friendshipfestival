Vendors & Shops

Wine Country Ontario

Our Wine Country Ontario sampling booth features six different VQA wines of Ontario each day! Guests are welcome to sample two complimentary one ounce samples of VQA wine of their choice!

Charlie's Chopsticks

With two locations, one in Hamilton and one in Welland, Charlie’s Chopsticks serves a healthy alternative to traditional fast foods and oriental cuisines. They specialize in customizable Sushi rolls, Rice bowls, and Bubble Tea. They will also be serving ice coffees at the Festival.

The Bald Baker Inc.

This Toronto-based bakery is dedicated to serving their customers sugar conscious, grain-free, egg-free, dairy-free, vegan, and worry-free deserts.

The Guilty Burger

Owner & operator Winston has a passion for food, but not just any food – yep, you got it…he’s got a passion for BURGERS!
